Research

Meteorological Prediction with Drosophila melanogaster

Designed and executed laboratory experiments to analyze common fruit fly agitation as a result of atmospheric disturbances. Determined the correlations between these patterns and thus used to predict future meteorological conditions.

Prenatal Alcohol Exposure of Drosophila melanogaster

Analyzed fruit fly alcohol tolerance by experimenting on flies with and without presence of the alcohol dehydrogenase gene. Research concluded that prenatal alcohol exposure does significantly affect offspring size and reproduction rates.

Infant and Computer Learning Strategies

Proposes an experiment surrounding the idea of infant-inspired machine learning tactics. By replicating the way in which infants learn to recognize, one could, in theory, improve the accuracy of AI object-recognition algorithms.

Outliers of the Spectrum: An Analysis of the Accuracy of the Highest SAT/IQ Scores

A discussion surrounding 'ceiling effects' among the highest scoring SAT and IQ test-takers. This research suggests the importance of new advanced exams in order to more accurately reflect the abilities of the highest scoring students.
